Professional Biography

Dr. Bo Yang is an established academic researcher, university research professor, and an elected Fellow of the Royal Society of Medicine (FRSM, UK). He currently serves as the Director of the Institute of Lipids Medicine at Wenzhou Medical University, China. Expanding his global scientific footprints, he is actively conducting tenure as a Visiting Researcher at the world-renowned Karolinska Institutet in Sweden (2025–2026).

With a highly robust scientific portfolio encompassing over 70 peer-reviewed articles and an active citation index of 2,980+ citations, Dr. Yang's research program sits at the cutting-edge interface of lipid nutrition, nutritional epidemiology, and nutritional psychiatry.

His laboratory bridges large-scale multi-centric cohorts (such as the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey - NHANES) with bioinformatics frameworks, network pharmacology, and selective, mechanisms-focused clinical translation. Nationally, he holds multiple senior leadership roles, including executive vice-chairmanships of eminent national nutrition and research bodies in China.
