Biography

I am a Professor specializing in Nutritional Epidemiology and Lipid Medicine at the Institute of Lipids Medicine, Wenzhou Medical University. Currently, I am expanding our global research footprint as a Visiting Scholar at the prestigious Karolinska Institutet (KI) in Sweden.

My research focuses on unraveling the complex, multi-faceted relationships between dietary lipids, metabolic health, and cognitive well-being. Throughout my academic career, my research program has yielded a robust portfolio of peer-reviewed publications in leading clinical and nutritional journals, establishing a highly-cited body of work with an h-index of 25.

Deeply committed to professional leadership and service, I serve as the Vice Chair of the Academic Committee for a national first-level academic society, and hold a membership with the Royal Society of Medicine (UK). I also active support the peer-review community as an editorial board member and referee for prominent international journals in the nutrition and public health domains.

Research Interests

My laboratory's scientific exploration sits at the crossroads of public health, metabolic medicine, and psychogeriatrics. We implement state-of-the-art methodology to investigate:

  • Lipid Medicine & Cardiovascular Health: Exploring the clinical and biological efficacy of omega-3 fatty acids and complex lipid profiles in mitigating cardiometabolic risks.
  • Nutritional Psychiatry & Gut-Brain Axis: Deciphering the diet-microbiome-microglia pathways and their crucial regulatory roles in neuroinflammation and cognitive decline (e.g., Alzheimer's disease).
  • Advanced Causal Inference Frameworks: Formulating and applying advanced epidemiological methods—including nested target trials, cloning methods, and E-value sensitivity analyses—to extract causal insights from large-scale longitudinal cohorts.
