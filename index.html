Biography

Dr. Bo Yang is an established academic researcher, university research professor, and an elected Fellow of the Royal Society of Medicine (FRSM). He currently serves as the Director of the Institute of Lipids Medicine at Wenzhou Medical University, and is undertaking an active tenure as a Visiting Researcher at the Karolinska Institute, Sweden.

With a robust portfolio encompassing over 70 peer-reviewed publications and a prominent citation profile (nearly 3,000 citations), Dr. Yang specializes in lipid nutrition, nutritional epidemiology, and nutritional psychiatry. His pioneering work bridges large-scale multi-centric population datasets (e.g., NHANES) with cutting-edge bioinformatics, network pharmacology frameworks, and selective laboratory experimental validations. He holds established national leadership roles as vice-chair across leading scientific councils in China and maintains proactive collaborations across international academic and medical networks.
